# Create LinkedIn Post Command

## Purpose
Generate engaging LinkedIn content optimized for professional networking, thought leadership, and audience engagement.

## Usage
```bash
claude create-linkedin-post [topic] [format] [options]
```

## Agent Assignment
**Primary Agent:** `~/.claude/agents/marketer.md`
**Supporting Agent:** `~/.claude/agents/designer.md` (for visual content)

## Workflow

### 1. Content Strategy
- Define post objective and target audience
- Choose optimal content format and style
- Research trending topics and hashtags
- Plan content calendar integration

### 2. Content Creation
- Craft attention-grabbing hook
- Develop valuable insights or commentary
- Structure for LinkedIn's algorithm preferences
- Create compelling call-to-action

### 3. Optimization & Enhancement
- Add relevant hashtags and mentions
- Include multimedia elements when appropriate
- Optimize for mobile viewing
- Schedule for optimal engagement times

### 4. Engagement Strategy
- Plan follow-up comment responses
- Identify engagement opportunities
- Create discussion starters
- Monitor performance metrics

## Content Formats

### Thought Leadership
- Industry insights and predictions
- Professional experience stories
- Lessons learned and best practices
- Commentary on industry trends

### Educational Content
- How-to guides and tutorials
- Tool recommendations and reviews
- Skill development tips
- Career advice and guidance

### Social Proof & Success Stories
- Client testimonials and case studies
- Project showcase and results
- Team achievements and milestones
- Award announcements and recognition

### Behind-the-Scenes
- Company culture and values
- Team spotlights and introductions
- Day-in-the-life content
- Workspace and process insights

## LinkedIn Best Practices
- Keep posts under 1,300 characters for optimal engagement
- Use line breaks for improved readability
- Include 3-5 relevant hashtags
- Ask questions to encourage comments
- Tag relevant people and companies
- Post during business hours for maximum reach

## Engagement Tactics
- Use storytelling frameworks (problem/solution, before/after)
- Include personal anecdotes and experiences
- Ask thought-provoking questions
- Create carousel posts for complex topics
- Share valuable resources and tools

- LinkedIn post copy with formatting
- Hashtag strategy and recommendations
