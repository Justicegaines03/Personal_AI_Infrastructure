# Author Wisdom Command

## Purpose
Extract key insights, methodologies, and wisdom from influential authors, thought leaders, and industry experts for personal and professional development.

## Usage
```bash
claude author-wisdom [author-name] [focus-area] [output-format] [options]
```

## Agent Assignment
**Primary Agent:** `~/.claude/agents/marketer.md`
**Supporting Agent:** `~/.claude/agents/designer.md` (for visual summaries)

## Workflow

### 1. Author Research & Profiling
- Research author background and expertise areas
- Identify key publications, books, and speeches
- Analyze thought leadership and industry influence
- Map author's evolution of ideas over time

### 2. Content Analysis & Synthesis
- Extract core philosophies and methodologies
- Identify recurring themes and patterns
- Analyze practical frameworks and tools
- Synthesize actionable insights and principles

### 3. Contextual Application
- Relate insights to current projects and challenges
- Adapt frameworks for modern contexts
- Identify implementation opportunities
- Create personalized action plans

### 4. Knowledge Packaging
- Create structured summaries and guides
- Design visual frameworks and infographics
- Develop practical worksheets and templates
- Organize insights by topic and application

## Extraction Categories

### Core Philosophy & Mindset
- Fundamental beliefs and worldview
- Problem-solving approaches and thinking patterns
- Decision-making frameworks and criteria
- Success principles and failure lessons

### Methodologies & Frameworks
- Step-by-step processes and systems
- Strategic planning and execution models
- Assessment tools and diagnostic frameworks
- Measurement and evaluation methods

### Practical Applications
- Case studies and real-world examples
- Implementation strategies and tactics
- Common pitfalls and how to avoid them
- Success stories and lessons learned

### Industry Insights
- Market analysis and trend predictions
- Competitive strategy and positioning
- Innovation and disruption patterns
- Future opportunities and challenges

## Output Formats

### Executive Summary
- Author background and expertise overview
- Top 10 key insights and principles
- Most actionable frameworks and tools
- Recommended reading and resources

### Detailed Analysis
- Comprehensive philosophy breakdown
- Historical context and evolution of ideas
- Critical analysis and potential limitations
- Comparative analysis with other thought leaders

### Practical Implementation Guide
- Step-by-step application instructions
- Customizable templates and worksheets
- Success metrics and evaluation criteria
- Timeline and milestone recommendations

### Visual Knowledge Map
- Mind maps and concept diagrams
- Framework visualizations and flowcharts
- Quote collections and inspiration boards
- Progress tracking and reflection tools
